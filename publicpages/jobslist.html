Waiter

What skills do I need?

As a waiter one of the most important things is to be charismatic and friendly with customers and a good listener.

 Being able to adapt to the situation and person is pivotal within this role, some customers are not the most conversational and wish for a fast and easy service. Some customers are highly talkative and enjoy engaging in small talk with whoever is serving them if possible. 

Additionally a good grasp of technology and good pendenship is important. We use tablets to record orders however Waiter's should always be prepared for when technology doesn't work
