BOOKING

Quality food should be measured by it's taste not price

Combining the notable and less notable delicious dishes from China. Dragons Garden seeks to bring the fullest of flavour at your table or straight to your door. From classics such as Chicken Balls, Fried rice and Duck Pancakes. To more elaborate dishes . Dragons Garden cooks every meal with fresh produce and ingredients, never from a freezer. All while giving a price fitting of what you pay for, never with a eye-watering mark up that makes you believe what your getting is what you paid for.

Instead making you taste that what your getting is what you paid for. Food priced on it's taste, not on it's looks or status.

Starting out as a small restuaunt on the street corner. Our company founder was estatic to see the people of West Midlands be so enthusiastic to go out their comfort zone and try foods across the globe. But was disappointed to see that instead of honoring such bold decisions, many buisnesses sought to take advantage with low quality food or mediocre food with a ridiculous price tag.

Working day and night to expand and improve, Dragons Garden went from a small corner shop to a full size restuaunt. Leading an example of the responsibility and passion that should be served in chinese food, and tasted with every bite.
